Player About

The player is written as a working model for educational purposes. The programming languages and technologies used are: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, API. Some tracks can't be played here due to the copyright. They are marked: 'The track can only be played on SoundCloud web-site'. If you want to play them, you have to go to SoundCloud web-site directly. To start the searching process you can also use the Enter button on your keyboard.
